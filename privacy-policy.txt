1. INTRODUCTION

GupShupGo ("we", "our", or "us") is an open-source, production-ready
Flutter communication application. This Privacy Policy explains how we
collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use
the GupShupGo mobile application ("App").

By using GupShupGo, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy
Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide
─────────────────────────────
- Phone Number: Used for OTP-based authentication via Firebase.
- Display Name & Profile Photo: Shown to other users within the app.
- Messages: Text messages and media you send to other users.
- Status Updates: Text, images, or videos you post as statuses.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically
────────────────────────────────────────
- Device Token (FCM): Used to deliver push notifications for incoming
  calls and messages. Stored in Firestore against your user profile.
- Online/Offline Status: Your presence status (online/offline) is
  updated in real-time to allow other users to see your availability.
- Call Logs: Type of call (audio/video), duration, timestamp, and
  call status (answered, missed, cancelled) are stored per user.
- Read Receipts: Message delivery and read timestamps are stored in
  Firestore to display seen/delivered indicators.

2.3 Media & Files
──────────────────
- Images, videos, and files you share in chats are uploaded to
  Firebase Storage and linked within Firestore messages.
- Status media (images/videos) is stored in Firebase Storage and
  automatically expires after 24 hours.

2.4 Guest Login
────────────────
- If you sign in as a Guest, Firebase creates an anonymous UID.
  No phone number or personal data is collected in this mode.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information collected solely to operate the App:

- Authenticate your identity and maintain your session.
- Deliver messages, calls, and notifications to you and recipients.
- Display your profile, online status, and statuses to other users.
- Maintain call log history for your reference.
- Enable real-time communication features via Agora RTC Engine.
- Provide technical support and resolve issues.

We do NOT use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or sell
it to any third parties.

5. DATA STORAGE & RETENTION

- All data is stored on Firebase infrastructure (Google Cloud).
  Servers may be located in any region supported by Firebase.
- Messages and call logs are retained until you delete them or your
  account is deleted.
- Status updates (media) are automatically deleted from Firebase
  Storage after 24 hours of posting.
- Expired status metadata may persist briefly in Firestore until
  the next cleanup cycle.
- Guest accounts may be deleted after prolonged inactivity.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6. DATA SECURITY

- Firestore security rules restrict data access so that only
  authenticated users can read/write their own data.
- Firebase Storage rules restrict file access to the intended sender
  and recipient.
- All data in transit is protected by TLS/HTTPS encryption.
- Agora RTC channels use encrypted audio/video streams.
- We recommend users enable device lock/biometrics for additional
  protection of their device.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. Use the App at your
own risk.

7. PERMISSIONS REQUESTED

GupShupGo requests the following device permissions:

Permission              | Purpose
────────────────────────|──────────────────────────────────────────────
CAMERA                  | Video calls and capturing status media
RECORD_AUDIO            | Voice/audio calls
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE   | Selecting images/videos from gallery
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE  | Saving received media files
POST_NOTIFICATIONS      | Incoming call and message notifications
FOREGROUND_SERVICE      | Handling calls while app is in background
INTERNET                | Connect to Firebase and Agora servers
WAKE_LOCK               | Keep device active during active calls

Permissions are only used for the stated purposes and are not accessed
in the background beyond what is required for core functionality.
